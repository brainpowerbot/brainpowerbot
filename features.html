Features List

These are some of the features that MessengerBot has! Feel free to try them out on the left hand column or drop down menu if on mobile.

  • Log into Facebook
  • Go to a friend's profile
  • Reset your Facebook password
  • Block a person
  • Check your Facebook privacy settings
  • Get a friend's and/or my basic info

Log into Facebook

Some cognitive impairments we focused on revolve around difficulty with memories. Our bot can help navigate Facebook's platform by giving simple instructions on how to do things. This feature helps people log into Facebook. Since user privacy is something we think is very important, we will never ask the user to input or store their username or password. This just serves as a tutorial on how to log into Facebook in the case that they ever get signed out of their account. You can ask the following of the bot to get help logging into Facebook.

  • "Help me log into Facebook"
  • "How do I login?"

Go to a friend's profile

Some cognitive impairments we focused on revolve around difficulty with memories and understanding complex language. Our bot can help navigate Facebook's platform by giving simple instructions on how to do things. Facebook's platform could be confusing to navigate, for some people, and this feature helps by telling the user multiple ways of getting to their friend's profile. You can ask the following of the bot to get help going to a friend's profile page

  • "Can you take me to my friend's profile page?"
  • "How to go to a friend's profile page?"

Reset your Facebook password

Some cognitive impairments we focused on revolve around difficulty with memories. Our bot can help navigate Facebook's platform by giving simple instructions on how to do things. This feature helps people reset their Facebook password. Since user privacy is something we think is very important, we will never ask the user to input or store their username or password. This just serves as a tutorial on how to reset their password in the case that they forget their password and/or the user feels like their account has been hacked. You can ask the following for our bot to get help resetting your Facebook password.

  • "I forgot my password, can you help?"
  • "Could you help me reset my password?"

Block a person

Some cognitive disabilities can make detecting malicious efforts from others difficult. Sometimes people just really want to block someone. If people feel uncomfortable with someone on Facebook, they should know the necessary steps to block that person if necessary. This feature guides the user, step by step, on how to block somebody. You can ask the following to get help blocking somebody.

  • "How do I block someone?"
  • "Can you help me block a person?"

Check your Facebook privacy settings

Privacy is important for many people with cognitive impairment. It is non-trivial to ensure that a facebook page is set to the appropriate levels of privacy for the user. Some reasons a user with a cognitive disability might wish to have more privacy than the average user include imparement in judgement of unknown user intent. Brainpower bot can help the user by walking them through setting their privacy settings on Facebook. You can ask the following of the bot to get privacy help.

  • "Help me set privacy settings"
  • "How can I make my posts private?"

Get a friend's and/or my basic info

This feature helps with retreiving basic information like posts and friends lists. With this information, the user can then use our language services to determine if a post is positive or not. The friends list can also be used to check if the user knows a certain person or not, since some cognitive disabilities makes remembering people very difficult. You can ask the following to begin getting people's basic information.

  • "Can you get my friends list?"
  • "Can you get my friend's posts?"
  • "Can you get my posts?"
