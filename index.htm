About the project

Our chatbot is a research project designed to help people, especially those with cognitive disabilities, access Facebook. We believe that everybody should be connected with friends and family, regardless of cognitive ability, and this chatbot is a step towards that direction. Hopefully Facebook can take a look at our bot and see if it is something they are willing to implement into their live site so that this can then achieve our goal of helping millions of people. With the guidance of CU Coleman Institute for Cognitive Disabilities, our Messenger chatbot can help with tasks that could be challenging for some people to do. The following website is a resource showcasing the research we conducted, and allowing users to interactively play with our chatbot! Please feel free to interact with our chatbot, and explore what is currently possible, our research, and the lessons we learned while taking part in this project

As a side note: since we are using a testing environment for this bot as a proof of concept, the only user that is currently logged into the bot is Sharon McNamara. Also, our bot gets "smarter" the more people use it so please feel free to play around with our bot! If you have any feedback just type "feedback" and the bot will record whatever it is you type next for our records.
